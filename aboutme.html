About Me

Hi, Welcome to the 'About Me' section. Hello, my name is Arian Kharazmi. I am an Ohioan, born in 2002, and I am currently 20 years old, studying Information Technology at the University of Cincinnati. I am hoping to pursue a Bachelor's degree, and a Master's degree in the long term, it will depend on where I am in the future. Here are some things you should know about me. My main interests are best listed as: Technology, Activism, and the ideals of self-wellbeing.

Technology

I find technology very interesting, as there is so much to do with it. Primarily it has been/is being used to improve people's lives, but it is also very interesting to see what we can do with it. Being a part of projects where we worked with programming Raspberry Pis, or even just being able to see what new technologies come out that can push the boundaries.

Activism

I find activism as one of my interests because I always felt like everyone has a voice to be shared. You don't have to organize a huge rally, or be a well-known organizer. But marching and fighting for something you believe in gives you purpose. It gives you a sense that you can make a difference, no matter how small your voice can be, you have no idea the impact it can make when others join your call. Like a harmony, you can use your voice to fight for what you believe in. One must always remember that they are a precious soul, with a voice, and no one can ever take that from you.

Ideals of well-being

The importance of self well-being cannot be understated. Understanding your own true worth is one of the most( sometimes underrated) important ideals one can have. In this era, the era of COVID-19, and the Great Resignation, I believe that many people around us, in all sorts of positions--even high level executives--are starting to realize their own self worth and respect their well-being. This is one of the most important interests, the most important overall one here. This interest is important, whether you are a simple office worker, or an important executive.

Future

I hope to look back on this website in the future and remember the times I spent to work on this or a project. I am pursuing that dream career in IT/CS because I have always wanted to use technology for the ability to push communities forward and change someone’s life you don’t even know for the better. I want to be involved in the decisions that can help our world progress forward. My dream is to someday have my own tech start-up company.
